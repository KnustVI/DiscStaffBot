1. ABOUT THE BOT AND ITS CURRENT STATE:

The Bot is in testing ("beta") phase – Instability, bugs, service outages, or temporary loss of configuration data or logs may occur, with or without prior notice.

Titan's Pass brings together, today or in development, the following main areas:

• Moderation: punishments/strikes with a reputation system, temporary roles, infraction logging;
• Reports: a ticket/report-chat system for reports between members, with service rating;
• Staff analytics: activity and performance metrics for the moderation team;
• Game server integration (RCON and webhooks): receives events from the Path of Titans server (login/logout, combat, etc.) and lets server staff send administrative commands to the game through the Bot;
• Player profiles: display of player statistics (playtime, reputation, Alderon ID) with customizable cards (avatar, background, badge);
• Web admin panel (dashboard): configuration interface parallel to Discord commands;
• Premium system: free and paid tiers with additional features, described in Section 5.

During the testing phase, all features are free. Once testing ends, the Bot will operate under two models:

• Free version: limited features;
• Premium version: additional features, via subscription or one-time payment (to be defined).

5. STORE, CURRENCY, AND PREMIUM SYSTEM:

Titan's Pass has its own internal economy, using a virtual currency called Hunt. Players can earn Hunt through special events in partnership with the server, giveaways, missions, and playtime — with higher earning rates depending on Premium tier.

[==The Bot's store offers, and intends to continue offering, only profile-customization items within Titan's Pass itself==]{#66ff66} (avatar, background, badge, title, and similar), with no effect, advantage, or unlock within the Path of Titans game. No amount of Hunt, item, or benefit acquired or earned through the Bot alters attributes, progress, growth, items, or any other aspect of the game itself.

Should the Bot later offer a skins project through mods, that content will be made available in compliance with Alderon Games' Modding Agreement, which prohibits selling or "paywalling" mods/UGC and prohibits granting in-game skins or benefits as a return for a donation or payment. No such feature will go live before that compliance is guaranteed.

Premium tiers (Free, Rastreador/Tracker, Caçador/Hunter for servers; Free, Compy, Raptor for players) grant access to tools, automations, higher limits, and customization — never advantages within the Path of Titans game itself. Details for each tier are described in the Bot's Premium reference document, available on the support server.

6. PRIVACY POLICY – DATA COLLECTED:

To operate moderation, reports, staff analytics, and game-integration features, the Bot may collect and store the following data:

• User, role, channel, and message IDs — reference required for punishments and logs;
• Message content — only when explicitly reported or configured for automatic moderation;
• History of commands executed by staff — audit trail (who, when, which action);
• Infraction records — punished user, reason, date, responsible moderator;
• Server (guild) ID;
• Alderon ID (player identification within Path of Titans);
• Game statistics linked to the Alderon ID — playtime, online/offline status, and events received via the server's webhook;
• Custom Bot settings;
• Discord username/nickname;
• Identification in logs and punishments;
• Images uploaded by the user for profile customization (avatar/background/badge).

The Bot does [==NOT==]{#ff4444} collect or store:

• Passwords, tokens, real e-mail addresses, phone numbers;
• Banking or credit card data;
• Unreported private messages (DMs);
• Voice or video call content;
• Path of Titans account data (login, password, progress) beyond the Alderon ID;
• Discord account data (login, password) beyond the user ID.
